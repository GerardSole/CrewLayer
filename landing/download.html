System requirements

macOS 12 Monterey or later · Intel or Apple Silicon
Windows Windows 10 version 1903 or later · 64-bit
Linux Ubuntu 20.04+, Debian 11+, or compatible · x86_64
RAM 4 GB minimum · 8 GB recommended
Disk 1 GB for app · additional space for agent data
Internet (first run) Required to download PostgreSQL binaries (~50 MB, one-time)
